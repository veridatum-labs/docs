---
title: "Proof Types"
description: "Supported and planned EarnProof proof types"
---

## Minimum Income Proof

Proves total or average income met or exceeded a threshold during a selected period.

## Recurring Income Proof

Proves qualifying payments were received at a required frequency.

## Payment Receipt Proof

Proves a particular payment occurred.

## Planned Proof Types

- Income range proof
- Employer payment proof
- Employment continuity proof
- Invoice settlement proof
- Aggregate earnings proof

## Future Cryptographic Proofs

Future versions may support:

- greater-than proofs,
- range proofs,
- count proofs,
- consecutive-month proofs,
- aggregate proofs,
- confidential token workflows.
