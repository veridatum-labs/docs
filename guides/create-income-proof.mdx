---
title: "Create an Income Proof"
description: "Generate a selective income credential from qualifying payments"
---

EarnProof supports three initial proof types.

## Minimum Income Proof

Proves that total or average income met a threshold during a selected period.

Example:

> The subject received at least 1,000 USDC monthly between January and March 2026.

## Recurring Income Proof

Proves that qualifying payments were received at a required frequency.

Example:

> The subject received at least one qualifying payment every month for six consecutive months.

## Payment Receipt Proof

Proves that a specific payment occurred.

Example:

> A payment of an undisclosed amount was received from an approved source on July 2, 2026.

## Proof Creation Flow

1. Select proof type.
2. Select date range.
3. Select asset.
4. Select eligible payment sources or transactions.
5. Enter proof condition.
6. Review disclosure preview.
7. Confirm proof creation.
8. Receive proof ID, credential JSON, QR code, and verification URL.

## Disclosure Preview

The user should see:

- disclosed claim,
- hidden fields,
- expiration date,
- trust level,
- issuer,
- network,
- asset,
- whether exact amount is revealed,
- whether source transactions are revealed,
- whether sender is revealed.
