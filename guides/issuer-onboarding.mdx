---
title: "Issuer Onboarding"
description: "Register organizations that can create higher-trust attestations"
---

Issuers are organizations that can back payment or employment claims.

## Issuer Application

An organization submits:

- display name,
- website,
- contact details,
- public metadata,
- issuer Stellar address.

## Admin Review

Maintainers review issuer applications and decide whether to approve, suspend, reactivate, or revoke issuers.

## On-Chain Registration

Approved issuers can be registered in the issuer registry:

```rust
register_issuer(issuer_id_hash, issuer_address, metadata_hash)
```

## Trust Levels

Issuer-backed proofs should be visually distinguished from self-generated proofs. Verifiers should understand what the issuer did and did not attest.
